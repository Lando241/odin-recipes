Soft Baked White Chocolate Macadamia Nut Cookies

These soft baked cookies are loaded with flavor.

Ingreditents

  • 2 cups & 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg & 1 egg yolk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup roughly chopped macadamia nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 F.
  2. Whisk flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl. Set aside./li>
  3. Wisk melted butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract until combined.
  4. Pour into dry ingredients and mix until completely combined.
  5. Fold in the white chocolate chops and macadamia nuts.
  6. Cover and chill the dough in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  7. Allow to sit at room temperature for at least 20 minutes before rolling and baking.
  8. Roll cookie dough into balls, approx 1-1.5 tablespoons per cookie.
  9. Arrange cookies 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 12-13 minutes or until likely browned on the sides.
  11. Remove from the oven and allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes.
  12. Transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.

Grilled Reuben Sandwich

Classic Reuben sandwich finshed off on a grill to add flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of rye bread
  • 2 slices of deli sliced corn beef
  • 2 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup of saurkraut, drained
  • 1/4 cup Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle on Medium heat.
  2. Lightly butter one side of the bread.
  3. Spread the non-buttered side with Thousand Island dressing.
  4. Layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices of corned beef, sauerkraut.
  5. Top with second slice of Swiss, and remaining bread slice, buttered side out.
  6. Grill sandwich until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
  7. Serve hot.
